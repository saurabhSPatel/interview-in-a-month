Flash Cards

How do you explain a trade-off in an interview without sounding vague?
Structure: "I'd choose X because [specific reason]. The cost is [specific downside]. I'd mitigate it with [specific mechanism]. I'd reconsider if [specific condition]." Never "it depends" without immediately saying what it depends on.
What makes an answer staff-level vs senior-level?
Staff: proactively surfaces failure modes, names operational knobs (TTL, partition count, timeout budget), proposes observability, states migration path, ties decisions to business constraints. Senior: answers questions correctly. Staff: drives the conversation.
