Foundations & Mental Models

CAP, Consistency, and Latency Budgeting

Distributed systems always trade consistency, availability, and partition tolerance. Your design quality improves when you specify the consistency contract per API and map each request into a clear latency budget.

05

Advanced + Interview Playbook (Days 28-30)

45-Minute Interview Structure

0-5 min: Clarify requirements, constraints, and SLOs.
5-12 min: Estimate scale and identify first-order bottlenecks.
12-25 min: Present high-level design with APIs and data model.
25-38 min: Deep dive on hardest component (consistency, partitioning, backpressure).
38-45 min: Failure modes, observability, and phased evolution plan.
